Foreword

The provisions of this Compliance Profile may be utilized by any interested parties, by reference to its URL, for the purpose of establishing feasibility demonstrations and tests of the provisions of IEC 61499-1 and 61499-2.

This document was originally developed in the form of a Technical Agreement by members of the Holonic Manufacturing Systems (HMS) Consortium to guide the development of the first inter-vendor feasibility demonstration of IEC 61499 at the International HMS Symposium in Kitakyushu City, Japan on October 19-20, 2000. The HMS Partners involved in the original development and testing of this specification were Rockwell Automation, Softing AG and Profactor GmbH. Following this demonstration, this document was updated to record the lessons learned regarding the requirements for successful implementation of IEC 61499.

The contents of Clauses 1-5 of this document are normative for its specified purposes; all Annexes are informative.

Revisions

  • A major revision of this Compliance Profile was performed at the end of March 2002 to meet the newly formulated requirements of IEC/PAS (Publicly Available Specification) 61499-4, and to incorporate additional lessons learned during the ongoing implementation of the publicly available FBDK software tool and its associated runtime platform (FBRT). Implementors of software tools, resources and devices desiring to achieve the attributes of software portability, interoperability, configurability and interchangeability with the FBDK and FBRT should ensure that their products conform to the corresponding normative requirements of this Compliance Profile.
  • A further revision of this Profile was performed in October 2006 to conform its contents to the provisions of IEC 61499-4: 2005. This consisted of:
    • Re-numbering of clauses and subclauses as prescribed by IEC 61499-4;
    • re-numbering of references to reflect the current organization of IEC 61499-1: 2005;
    • revision of Annex A to reflect more accurately this Profile's extensions to IEC 61499.
